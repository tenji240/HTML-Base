About

FASA is a collegiate cultural interest group that aims to promote Pilipino and Filipino-American awareness at UMBC and its surrounding community. Founded in 1997, FASA was established on the four principles of education, integration, charity, and family. Through the years, it has grown tremendously into one of the largest, most active, and well-known organizations on campus.
